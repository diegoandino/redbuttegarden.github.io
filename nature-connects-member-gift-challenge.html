NATURE CONNECTS: MEMBER GIFT CHALLENGE

Double the impact of your gift by donating to Red Butte Garden today!

For the next two weeks, donations made to Red Butte Garden's summer exhibition - Nature Connects®: Art with LEGO® Bricks will be matched, up to $2,000, by two generous Garden Members: Laurie Eastwood and Kym Buttschardt.

Inspired by nature and built from more than 450,000 LEGO bricks, this award-winning exhibition will feature 14 sculptures that represent the incredible connections that sustain life on Earth - including your own connection to nature!

Join Laurie and Kym to help fund the "Garden Community" sculpture featuring birds and squirrels, made of 8,586 LEGO bricks! By supporting this exciting exhibition, you are helping to share the Garden's mission to connect people with plants and the beauty of living landscapes with your community in a unique and engaging way.

Double your impact and give today.
