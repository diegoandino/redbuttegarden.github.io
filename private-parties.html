CORPORATE EVENTS & PRIVATE PARTIES

Plan your meeting, fundraiser, conference, or team-building event at Red Butte Garden. Open year-round, the Richard K. Hemingway Orangerie can be set up for a presentation, formal dinner, or open-house event. Our Classroom space is perfect for smaller meetings. The Rose House is designed to meet the needs of smaller meetings, luncheons, or parties.

During the warmer months, parties and corporate events may be held in select outdoor areas of the Garden or Amphitheatre. In the winter, make your holiday party even more festive by holding it the snow-covered wonderland of Red Butte Garden.

Contact Us

For inquiries about renting space in the Garden, reserving a time for an event, and any other event rental questions, please contact our Event Rentals Department at 801-585-9563 or by email at orangerie@redbutte.utah.edu
