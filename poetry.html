POETRY IN THE GARDEN

Red Butte Garden displays a number of Poetry Boxes scattered throughout the garden. Each poem is inspired by nature, and you can view the box locations on the map below.


Current Poems by Location


Be inspired by the natural world! The Garden hosts poetry boxes throughout the year, exhibiting both well known and lesser known poetry fitting with the changing seasons. Five poems are on display in the summer, fall, and winter. Eleven poems are exhibited during the spring with special "Poetry Month" programming happening during April.
