RED BUTTE GARDEN & NATURAL HISTORY MUSEUM OF UTAH EXPERIENCE

 
 



Are you interested in having your students enjoy both a plant-filled field class at Red Butte Garden and a dino-mite self-guided field trip at the Natural History Museum of Utah? Book your RBG/NHMU experience today! This experience is offered to K-4th grades with a minimum of 80 students and a maximum capacity of 120 students.

The Experience Includes:

  • Guided Red Butte Garden field class
  • Self-guided field trip at Natural History Museum of Utah
  • Reserved lunch space at NHMU*
  • Special activities to link your learning experience


RESERVE YOUR EXPERIENCE

*If you are interested in a same-day experience with RBG and NHMU, you must reserve a date. Times and dates are firm. If these times and/or dates do not work for your group, we will not be able to accommodate your request. Lunch space is only available at NHMU*

If you have less than 80 students, please contact each organization individually.
