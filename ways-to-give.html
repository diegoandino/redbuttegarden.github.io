Set up a Recurring Gift

Recurring gifts are an easy and efficient way to support your favorite Garden program or initiative. For as little as $5, you can provide a steady source of support for Red Butte Garden’s award-winning educational programs, springtime bulb display, or conservation research efforts. For more information on recurring gifts, click here here or call 801-587-9658.


Double the impact of your gift - see if your employer will match your donation!

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions or volunteer hours made by their employees. Visit the University of Utah Office of Development’s Matching Gift website to see if your company offers a matching gift program or contact your employers’ Human Resources Department.
