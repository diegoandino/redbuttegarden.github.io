Celebrate Arbor Day at the Garden! Join David Olszyk of the American Conifer Society and members of the Garden’s conifer planning committee on a tour to discuss the Garden’s amazing collection and how conifers can add interest to your winter landscape.

Bring your conifer-related questions and leave with a free tree to take home due to the generous support of Rocky Mountain Power.

Tour meets in the Courtyard Garden. Space limited to 20 guests.

Any remaining spots available on day of event on a first come, first served basis.
