Partner Camp with Clark Planetarium

Ready for an out of this world adventure? Spend your mornings at Red Butte Garden discovering the connection between space and the plants and animals found on planet Earth. From plants that grow without sunlight to real-life, alien-like creatures, you’ll discover the plethora of bizarre plants and animals from Earth that would be right at home on an alien planet. Spend your afternoons at the Clark Planetarium, exploring the extreme circumstances of space and how life could survive there. Discover how astronauts survive in space and experiment with the technology they use to explore the solar system. Transportation between sites provided.
