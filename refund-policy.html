REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICIES

Membership Discount

Garden Members: PLEASE SIGN IN HERE before adding education programs to your shopping cart. Your Member Discount will be reflected at checkout. If your membership is lapsed at time of purchase, price adjustment refunds will be given up to 5 business days from the time of purchase with a membership renewal. Members must contact Red Butte Garden’s Program Registrar in order to receive a tuition price adjustment within the 5 day timeframe. Refunds will not be given after 5 business days.

Summer Camps

  • 10 business days' cancellation notice is required to receive a full refund (less 20% processing fee).
  • NO refunds with less than 10 business days' notice.
  • No refund for missed days of camp.
  • Campers asked to withdraw from camp for Camper Code of Conduct violations will receive no refund.
  • Transfers into available camps will be granted up to 10 business days before the start of camp (space dependent).
  • Campers unable to attend due to medical reasons may receive a full refund (less 20% processing fee) at any time prior to the start of camp with a doctor’s note.
  • Camp registration fees are nontransferable.
  • The Garden reserves the right to cancel any camp due to low enrollment.
  • Camps cancelled by the Garden will automatically be fully refunded.

Garden Adventure Classes

  • All sales final.
  • No refund will be given for cancellations or missed classes.
  • Registration fees may be transferred to another available Garden Adventure class with at least 5 business days' notice.
  • Classes cancelled by the Garden will automatically be fully refunded.

Lil' Buds Classes

  • 10 business days' notice of cancellation is required to receive a full refund (less 20% processing fee).
  • NO refunds with less than 10 business days' notice.
  • Registration fees may be transferred to another availableLil' Buds class with at least 5 business days' notice.
  • Classes cancelled by the Garden will automatically be fully refunded.
  • Registration fees are nontransferable.
  • The Garden reserves the right to cancel any class due to low enrollment.

Adult Classes

  • Full payment is required at the time of registration.
  • Two week’s notice of cancellation is required to receive a full refund less a 20% processing fee.
  • NO refunds for no-shows.
  • Programs cancelled by the Garden will automatically be fully refunded.

Campout & Late Night

  • 10 business days' notice of cancellation is required to receive a full refund (less 20% processing fee).
  • NO refunds for no-shows.
  • Programs cancelled by the Garden will automatically be fully refunded.
