Shimmy 'n Sprout - NEW!

Partner Camp with Tanner Dance
JUNE 5 - 9
9AM TO NOON
GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: 5-6
SESSION: JUNE 5 - 9 (Monday-Friday)

Partner Camp with Tanner Dance

Explore the world of plants with Tanner Dance and Red Butte Garden. Start your day at the Garden where you’ll dig, plant, water, and weed your way across the plant kingdom. Later, you’ll work with Tanner Dance to learn more about plants and the connections they form in the natural world through dance and other movement activities. End the week with a special dance performance in the Garden showcasing all you’ve learned.

Caregivers: Please note the curriculum for Session 1 & 2 is the same.

Into the Woods

Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)
June 12-16
9AM - NOON at RBG
1 - 3PM at NHMU
GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: Ages 5 - 6
SESSION 1: JUNE 12 - 16 (Monday - Friday)

Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)

Ready to investigate the wonders of the natural world? Spend your mornings at Red Butte exploring the natural world with your senses as you plant seeds, search for birds, study animal habitats, make pet rocks, and more. In the afternoon, journey to NHMU to learn more about the plants, animals, and natural processes surrounding you through hands-on projects, science experiments, and excursions throughout the museum.

Drop off at Red Butte Garden by 9AM and pick up at NHMU by 4:30PM.

Click Here for Camp Overview.

NOTE: Registration for this class is done through NHMU

Junior Naturalists (3)

Partner Camp with NHMU
June 12-16
9AM-4:30PM
GRADE: 1st
AGES: 6-8
SESSION 1: JUNE 12 - 16
SESSION 2: JULY 31 - AUGUST 4

Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)

Explore the natural world with Red Butte Garden and the Natural History Museum of Utah! Spend mornings at Red Butte Garden looking at the natural world from the perspective of ants, raccoons, rocks, and more to discover the unique perspective each viewpoint gives you. In the afternoon travel to NHMU where you’ll investigate the natural processes surrounding you, including the changing seasons, the rock cycle, the water cycle, and more. Registration through NHMU.

Drop off at Red Butte Garden by 9AM and pick up at NHMU by 4:30PM.
