Partner Camp with Tanner Dance

Explore the world of plants with Tanner Dance and Red Butte Garden. Start your day at the Garden where you’ll dig, plant, water, and weed your way across the plant kingdom. Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)

Ready to investigate the wonders of the natural world? Spend your mornings at Red Butte exploring the natural world with your senses as you plant seeds, search for birds, study animal habitats, make pet rocks, and more. In the afternoon, journey to NHMU to learn more about the plants, animals, and natural processes surrounding you through hands-on projects, science experiments, and excursions throughout the museum.

Drop off at Red Butte Garden by 9AM and pick up at NHMU by 4:30PM.
