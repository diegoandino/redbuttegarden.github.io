GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: Ages 5 - 6

Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)

Ready to investigate the wonders of the natural world? Spend your mornings at Red Butte Garden exploring the natural world with your senses as you plant seeds, search for birds, study animal habitats, make pet rocks, and more. Cool down at NHMU in the afternoon and learn more about the plants, animals, and natural processes surrounding you through hands-on projects, science experiments, and excursions throughout the museum.
