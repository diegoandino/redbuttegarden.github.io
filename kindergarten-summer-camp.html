GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: 5-6

Partner Camp with Wasatch Community Gardens

Bug lovers, unite! Get up close and personal with the world of bugs as you explore the insects that call the Garden home. Through science experiments, insect-inspired art projects, real-life bug encounters, and more, you'll learn how insects see, study the butterfly lifecycle, and discover how insects help the Garden grow. Don't miss out on this bug-tastic camp!

JUNE 17 - 21
9AM - NOON
GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: Ages 5 - 6
SESSION: JUNE 17 - 21
Monday-Friday
9AM - NOON

Calling all bookworms! Explore the exciting world of books this week at camp as you read stories like Eating the Alphabet by Lois Ehlert, Bugs in the Garden by Beatrice Alemagna, and The Curious Garden by Peter Brown. After, you’ll complete fun art and science projects related to the day’s story like decorating flower pots, crafting egg carton caterpillars, and building DIY bug catchers. From hungry caterpillars to tea parties hosted by spiders you won’t want to miss this week of literary adventure!

Partner camp with Natural History Museum of Utah
JULY 29 - AUGUST 2
9AM - 3PM
GRADE: Kindergarten
AGES: 5 - 6
SESSION: JULY 29 - AUGUST 2
Monday - Friday
9AM - 3PM

Ready to investigate the wonders of the natural world? Spend your mornings at Red Butte Garden exploring the natural world with your senses as you plant seeds, search for birds, study animal habitats, make pet rocks, and more. Cool down at NHMU in the afternoon and learn more about the plants, animals, and natural processes surrounding you through hands-on projects, science experiments, and excursions throughout the museum.
