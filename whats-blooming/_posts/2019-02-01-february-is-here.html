Want to be surrounded by beauty and plants but don't want to go outside? You're in luck, come to Red Butte Garden and enjoy some time in the Orangerie, your eyes and nose will thank you.


Bird's Eyes     Gilia tricolor

These flowers sure are cute! Check out those blue stamens! That's where the pollen comes from. This is in our Greenhouse.


Echeveria     Echeveria cv.

These are quite the popular house plant and for good reason!


Tsubaki     Camellia japonica 'Debutante'

This flower exudes a delicious scent!


Pink Ice Orchid     Cymbidium 'Kirby Lesh Pink Ice'

Orchids have some of the coolest flowers!


Yellow Lady Banks' Rose     Rosa banksiae var. lutea

Here is a sneak peak of a rose in the Greenhouse. Look for it in the Garden in the future.


Whether you are feeling outdoorsy or want to keep warm inside, we have plenty to see up here at Red Butte Garden.
