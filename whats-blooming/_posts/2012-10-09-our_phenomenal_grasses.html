Fall is the best time to visit Red Butte Garden to see our extraordinary ornamental grass collection. Examples of our collection can be found in every garden. Be sure you don't miss seeing a single one.


Blue Grama Grass   Bouteloua gracilis 



Side Oats Grama Grass    Bouteloua curtipendula 



Tricolor Reed Canary Grass   Phalaris arundinacea  'Feesey Form'



Eulalia grass    Miscanthus sinensis  



The grass bed near the Water Pavilion highlights many of our amazing grasses in one location.
