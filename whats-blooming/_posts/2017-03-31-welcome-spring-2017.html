What a great time of year spring is. The blanket of snow melts and reveals the buds and blossoms we all look forward to.

Yes, it's that time! Our daffodils are up and looking fantastic!

Leonard Messel Magnolia    Magnolia x stellata 'Leonard Messel'

This beauty is hard to miss as you enter the Courtyard.

Silver Bouquet Lungwort    Pulmonaria 'Silver Bouquet'

A lover of shade, this perennial is planted under the trees along the upper Floral Walk.

Royal Star Magnolia    Magnolia stellata 'Royal Star'

As you stroll down the Floral Walk, these show stoppers are sure to catch your eye.

Wisley Daffodil    Narcissus 'Wisley'

Red Butte Garden is home to more than 160 cultivars of Daffodil. How many can you count?

Browse Milkvetch    Astragalus cibarius

Why not go for a hike in the Natural Area and enjoy our native flora?

The weather is getting warmer and the days longer, why not come check out what Red Butte has to offer.
