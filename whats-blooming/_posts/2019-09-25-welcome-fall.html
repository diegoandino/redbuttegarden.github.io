It's fall, a magical time of year in the Garden. Come enjoy the nice cool weather with the sun shining on your skin.


Vivid Obedient Plant     Physostegia virginiana 'Vivid'

If you move an individual flower on this plant, it will stay where you put it. That's an obedient plant!


Brilliant Sedum     Sedum x spectabile 'Brilliant'

If you are sensitive to nature you may want to look away. OR, you may want to look closely, that is a praying mantis eating a bee.


Blue Blazes Hyssop     Agastache 'Blue Blazes'

Both bees and humans alike enjoy this plant, as you can tell by the buzzing sound as you pass by, and the many photos people take of the plant.


Autumn Crocus     Colchicum autumnale

This autumn bloomer is a sure sign of fall.


Dragon Fly on Arrowleaf Arrowhead     Sagittaria cuneata

What big blue eyes you have dragon fly.


Ornamental Oregano     Origanum laevigatum 'Herrenhausen'

This plant displays unique flowers that the bees seem to love.
