Here is a sneak peek of behind the scenes and inside of our Greenhouses, which is where the magic begins at Red Butte Garden.

Greenhouse

Hart's Tongue Fern    Asplenium scolopendrium

This is the underside of a fern leaf. The tiny black round specks are spores, which can be thought of as seeds of a fern, and they are what will develop into a mature plant.

Venus Slipper    Paphiopedilum sukhakulii x roebelenii

Rain Tree    Brunfelsia sp.

This tree's flowers exude a pleasant sweet scent.

Chinese Hibiscus    Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

Moth Orchid    Phalaenopsis sp.

Mexican Flame Vine    Senecio confusus

Queen's Tears   Billbergia nutans
