Here is a sneak peak of behind the scenes and inside of our Greenhouses, which is where the magic begins at Red Butte Garden.
Greenhouse

Hart's Tongue Fern    Asplenium scolopendrium
This is the underside of a fern leaf. The tiny black round specks are spores, which can be thought of as seeds of a fern, and they are what will develop into a mature plant.
