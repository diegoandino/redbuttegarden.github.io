May is here and it's brought an exuberance of color!

Crown Imperial     Fritillaria imperialis

This majesty can be seen near the Water Pavilion. These flowers put off a skunky smell you probably won't be able to miss.


Autumn Amber Sumac     Rhus trilobata 'Autumn Amber'

This is a great choice for year round beauty and ground coverage.


Louisa Crabapple     Malus 'Louisa'

A fabulous weeping crabapple cultivar. On display in the Fragrance Garden.


Golden Currant     Ribes aureum

A fun Utah native that produces delicious edible fruit that ripen mid to late summer.


Painted Lady (Vanessa cardui) Butterfly on Burkwood Viburnum     Viburnum x burkwoodii

Even the pollinators are out!


Viking Village in the Childrens Garden

If you have kids, come check out our new Viking Village Display in the Childrens Garden.
