I think we're FINALLY in springtime.... The daffodils are finishing up and the lilacs, crabapples and viburnums are almost in full swing.



Volcan Lilac



Charles Holetich Lilac



Firmament Lilac



Beauty of Moscow Lilac



Lace-Leaf Lilac



Chokecherry

Look for this beautiful native throughout the garden and natural area; specifically along the north side of the pathway along the Great Wall and near the Water Pavillion.



Fragrant Viburnum

On the Floral Walk near the path the goes to the Rose Garden overlook, you'll find this very fragrant Viburnum.



Psgan Yellowhorn

This unique shrub can be found in the center of the Ring Garden of the Rose Garden.



Wisterias are just opening up....visit within the next couple weeks to catch the incredible display these flowers put on.
