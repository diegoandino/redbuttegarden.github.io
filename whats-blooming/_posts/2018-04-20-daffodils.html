We have quite the magnificent display of daffodils throughout the Garden right now. Come see the unique diversity!


Tahiti Daffodil    Narcissus 'Tahiti'

Passionale Daffodil    Narcissus 'Passionale'

Curly Lace Daffodil    Narcissus 'Curly Lace'

High Society Doffodil    Narcissus 'High Society'

...but wait! Daffodils are not the only bloomers in the Garden right now...


Ballhead Waterleaf    Hydrophyllum capitatum

This Utah native is a favorite! It is a member of the waterleaf family. The flowers hang below the leaves, so they may be a little harder to spot. They can be seen in the Natural Area, as well as under the oaks near the Northwest end of the Orangerie building.


Princess Kay Plum    Prunus nigra 'Princess Kay'

This is just one of the many trees of the rose family that are blossoming right now. It can be seen in the Rose Garden, and you might smell it before you see it!


Make sure to come up to the Garden for our famous Daffodil Display! While you're here, your nose will be greeted with many pleasant smells from all of the new blossoms in the Garden.
