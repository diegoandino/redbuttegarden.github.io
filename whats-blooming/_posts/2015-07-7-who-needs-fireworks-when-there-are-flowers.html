What do fireworks and flowers have in common? Bright beautiful colors! Come see our dazzling show of coloration the way nature intended, and prepare to be mesmerized!


Red-Hot PopsicleTM Poker  Kniphofia   'Red Hot Popsicle'

Walking through the Rose Garden you won't be able to miss this firecracker of a flower from the Aloe family.



Henry's Clematis  Clematis  'Henryi'

This vine doesn't need bright color to leave you star struck, it's pure white petals are sure to leave a lasting impression.



Munro's Globemallow    Sphaeralcea munroana 

Small but not to be missed, this Utah native is a favorite for its brilliant apricot-orange blooms.



Crane's Bill    Geranium  'Rozanne'

For such small blooms, this gorgeous perennial creates a sea of purple attracting animals and humans alike.



Red Yucca    Hesperaloe parviflora 

And for the grand finale, this Agave family member is a show stopper with high marks from hummingbirds as they cannot stay away.



Come see for yourself the vibrant show of summer blooms today!
