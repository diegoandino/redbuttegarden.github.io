After your morning parades and Fourth of July breakfasts, come up to Red Butte Garden with your picnic lunch and escape the busyness of the city to explore the gardens and see the beautiful plant displays. There are gorgeous flowers blooming in every corner of the Garden.


Jacob Cline Beebalm  Monarda didyma  'Jacob Cline'



Pineleaf Penstemon    Penstemon pinifolius 



Ryan Gainey Hydrangea    Hydrangea arborescens 'Ryan Gainey'



African Daisy    Osteospermum  'Avalanche'



Big Blue Sea Holly     Eryngium x zabelli  'Big Blue'



Royal Aspirations Larkspur     Delphinium elatum  'Royal Aspirations'



When you come to visit the Garden this Fourth of July holiday, see how many flowers you can find that represent our country's national colors.
