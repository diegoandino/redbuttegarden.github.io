The Garden is bursting at the seams with flowers blooming everywhere you look, so it's pretty easy to overlook some unique plants.


Ladyslipper Orchid   Cypripedium parviflorum var. pubescens 

Hidden in a sea of other awesome plants, this gorgeous orchid is growing in the shade of the crabapples near the Papa Moose sculpture.



Yellow Bishop's Hat  Epimedium x. versicolor  'Sulphureum'

Look for this handsome little fella' growing low in the Service Berry Tunnel on the Floral Walk.



Vegetable Sheep    Raoulia australis 

This member of the aster family has leaves less than 1/16" long and grows into large white mounds that can dot the hillside in their native Australia resembling sheep from a distance.



Crane's Bill    Geranium  'Rozanne'

For such small blooms, this gorgeous perennial creates a sea of purple attracting animals and humans alike.



Red Yucca    Hesperaloe parviflora 

And for the grand finale, this Agave family member is a show stopper with high marks from hummingbirds as they cannot stay away.



Come see for yourself the vibrant show of summer blooms today!
