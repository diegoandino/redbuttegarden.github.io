It is definitely getting warm outside and our plants are loving it. There are flowers blooming in every corner of the Garden.

Thompson's Yucca    Yucca thompsonii

Stately Yuccas greet you when you first enter the Water Conservation Garden.


With each passing day, the Water Conservation Garden mature a little more. Be sure to visit often to see what new surpises pop up.
