All this rain water is bringing a lot of color to the Garden, it might as well be raining flowers!


Jacobs Ladder     Polemonium caeruleum ssp. himalayanum

These are sure to brighten a rainy day! They can be admired in the Fragrance Garden.


Bleeding Heart     Dicentra spectabilis

One of my favorites, I can't help but smile when I pass by these heart shaped flowers along the Floral Walk.


Witch Alder     Fothergilla gardenii

These unique aromatic flowers can be seen in the Water Pavilion.


Rose Garden Waterfall

The lower pond and waterfall are looking quite stunning right now.


Betony     Stachys lavandulifolia

This furry floriferous groundcover is sure to grab your attention.


Next break in the clouds, come on up to the Garden and see all the flowers these May showers have brought.
