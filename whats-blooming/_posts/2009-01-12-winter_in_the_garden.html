If you haven't had the chance to visit the garden this winter, now is the perfect time. The snow really brings out the colors within the garden.

Liriodendron tulipifera Dried fruit with snow

Everything stands out against the backdrop of white, from the dark greens of the conifers to the red of the sandstone walls.

Our hard working garden staff does an excellent job at keeping all of the paths in the garden snow and ice free. So come take advantage of this perfect opportunity to see the garden and take some great photos.. Be sure to keep an eye out for our some of our elusive garden visitors such as bobcats and coyotes.
