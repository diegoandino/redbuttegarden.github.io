Now that it's spring and the weather is warmer, new flowers are popping up all over the place!


Grape Galaxy Hellebore     Helleborus 'Grape Galaxy'

These flowers like to hang their heads, keep your eyes low to see them!


Glory of the Snow     Chionodoxa forbesii 'Blue Giant'

These cuties can be seen at the bottom of the Floral Walk.


Floral Walk

Pretty soon this little hillside (as well as much of the Garden) will be covered in... daffodil flowers!


Quaking Aspen     Populus tremuloides

These are the flowers, aka catkins, from the quaking aspen tree along the Floral Walk.


With the warmimg temperatures, it is fun seeing what will pop up next! Come see for yourself!
