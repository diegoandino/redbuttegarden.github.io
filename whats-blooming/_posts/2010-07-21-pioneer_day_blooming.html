The garden is still alive with blossoms. There isn't anywhere in the garden you won't find beautiful, interesting and fragrant blossoms.


Bear's Breeches Acanthus spinosus

You can see Bear's Breeches growing in the Rose Garden on the pathway around the wedding lawn.


Bottlebrush Buckeye Aesculus parviflora

This gorgeous shrub can be found hidden along the Fern Gully trail on the Floral Walk.


Double Delight Rose Rosa 'ANDeli'

This is one of the most fragrant roses I have found in the garden. Exposure to UV light stimulates the petals to turn red. Look for this unique rose along the south path in the Rose Garden.
