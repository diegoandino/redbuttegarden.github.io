The Garden has been closed for the holidays, but here is a look of the Garden on the last day of 2019. The Garden opens back up on January 2, 2020.


Wild Turkeys in the Four Seasons Garden

We have some flighty visitors spending their holiday here at the Garden.


Winter Flame Dogwood     Cornus sanguinea 'Winter Flame'

This might be the brightest plant in the Garden right now!


Rose Garden Waterfall

Such a serene scene. One of my favorite spots to photograph.


Visitors Center Succulent Wall

It's cold outside, luckily here at Red Butte Garden, we have beauty on the inside as well. This wall is bursting with succulent life.


Visitors Center

This wondrous display makes me want to stay a while.


Bougainvillea     Bougainvillea 'Rosenka'

This bright pink flowered beauty can be seen in the Orangerie.


Remember, we shovel our walks for your walking pleasure, so if staying fit and healthy is one of your new year resolutions, make Red Butte a regular stop, your body and mind will thank you.
