Wow! Since last week the garden has really exploded with blossoms. From short groundcovers to massive trees, many plants have chosen now to begin flowering. You can't go anywhere in the garden without seeing a great amount of color everywhere you look.

Bugleweed Ajuga reptans 'Catlin's Giant'


Eastern Redbud Cercis canadensis 'Forest Pansy'


Leopard's Bane Doronicum orientale 'Little Leo'


Georgenberg Geum Geum x heldreichii 'Georgenberg'


Indian Summer Crabapple Malus 'Indian Summer'


Yellow Delicious Apple Malus x 'Yellow Delicious'


Calocarpa Crabapple Malus x zumi var. calocarpa


Sensation Lilac Syringa vulgaris 'Sensation'


Fragrant Viburnum Viburnum x carlcephalum


Keep an eye out for these flowers and many more in the weeks to come. For those of you that visit the garden to see our Lilac collection in bloom, this week and next will be the best time for that.
