The daffodils are as beautiful as ever! There are so many different types, sizes and colors it's hard to imagine without actually seeing them. My favorite is the kind with the orange throat.







If you thought you saw a lot of daffodils in the Four Seasons Garden, you should take a stroll down the Floral Walk. Both sides of the path is lined with the amazing blossoms.


With the burst of color from the daffodils, don't forget to stop and look at the native beauties in the Natural Area.

Astragalus utahensis Locoweed


Lomatium triternatum 9-Leaf Biscuitroot
