Spring is an exciting time at Red Butte Garden. We all look forward to seeing the impressive display of daffodils in just about every corner of the Garden.

You can walk almost anywhere in the Garden right now and be surrounded by a sea of yellow.



Ice Follies Daffodil    Narcissus  'Ice Follies'



Pineapple Prince Daffodil   Narcissus  'Pineapple Prince'



Minnow Daffodil     Narcissus  'Minnow'



Itzim Daffodil     Narcissus   'Itzim'



Our beautiful Magnolia trees are also beginning to bloom and they smell delightful.


Leonard Messel Magnolia     Magnolia x loebneri   'Leonard Messel'



You can find our various Magnolias growing in the Courtyard, Floral Walk, Medicinal Garden and now in the Gift Shop Patio.

If you can, you really should visit Red Butte Garden at least once a week to be able to appreciate the amount and the diversity of daffodils we have growing. Just as one cultivar closes, another completely different one will open.
