If you are a fan of hardy lavender, now is the time to visit! The gardens are filled with the enticing aroma. The best places to see lavender at Red Butte are in the Fragrance Garden and the Four Seasons Garden.


Hidcote Lavender Lavandula angustifolia 'Hidcote'



Irene Doyle Lavender Lavandula angustifolia 'Irene Doyle'



People aren't the only ones who love lavender. They are very good at attracting a wide variety of pollinators such as bees and butterflies.



While you are here, be sure to walk through the Rose Garden. All the plants are becoming more established and are looking fantastic! Now is a good time to see our roses in full bloom and decide which cultivars you want in your home garden.
