Come celebrate Pioneer Day at the garden where summer is in full bloom!


Daylily   Hemerocallis 'Fire Tree' 

Along the Floral Walk, this lily screams excitement with its deep red colored flowers.



Blue Glow Globe Thistle   Echinops bannaticus  'Blue Glow'

Even the bees are buzzing with exhilaration for these showy thistle blooms.



Ornamental Pepper    Capsicum annuum Black Pearl'

While in the Herb Garden, this small, but eye catching annual will bring you to your hands and knees in order to get a good view of its flowers' grandeur.



Desert Willow   Chilopsis linearis 

What a great way to commemorate Pioneer Day by coming to see this beautiful Utah native tree in flower. Its blooms are sure to elate your eyes with its ruffled pink petals.



Grecian Foxglove    Digitalis lanata 

This foxglove and its spikes of numerous flowers will ironically make your heart skip a beat with its fantastically light yellow to cream colored petals with brown veins.



Don't miss out on all the blooming excitement! Come visit Red Butte Garden to enhance your Pioneer Day enjoyment.
