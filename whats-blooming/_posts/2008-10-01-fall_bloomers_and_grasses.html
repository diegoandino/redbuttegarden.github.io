The grasses are in bloom or seed everywhere in the garden. Be sure to walk down to the Water Pavillion and check out the newly designed grass flowerbed.

If you want to see the Toad Lilys in bloom they are in Fern Gully. "How do I get there" you ask? Simple, if you are walking down the Floral Walk from the visitors center, take the dirt path to your left right before you get to the pear arbors.
