LIL' BUDS CLASSES

Preschool Preview

Day camps for preschool children (ages 3-4) and a caregiver.


Give your lil’ bud a jump start on preschool topics all while having a fun time with new friends. Through games, crafts and garden explorations you can help your lil’ bud discover how fun learning can be!
