Garden After Dark 2015

Legends of Camelot: Calling all Princesses, Knights & Wizards!

October 22, 23, 24 & 29 from 6-9PM
October 30 from 6-10PM

Journey to a realm of myth and magic as you visit the land of King Arthur and his Knights of the Round Table. Although you may have heard of Guinevere, Lancelot, and Merlin, you may not realize the large part the natural world plays in their stories. Explore the Garden and discover more about nature’s hidden ties to the people and places of Camelot.

With crafts, activities, light displays, and more, this is an event you definitely don’t want to miss! Garden guests will also have the opportunity to experience the story of Camelot through a special immersive dance-theater performance happening each evening. Become a part of the legend and join us for Garden After Dark, an indoor/outdoor, costume, and kid-friendly experience in the Garden to celebrate Halloween.
