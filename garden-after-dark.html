Garden After Dark: Adventures in Wonderland

October 19-21 & 26-28, 6 to 9PM

FAQ

Do adults need to purchase Garden After Dark tickets?
Yes. Everyone over the age of 2 is required to have a ticket to enter the event. Don’t forget children two and under are free!


Can I purchase tickets ahead of time?
You can pre-purchase tickets online below or by phone at (801) 581-8454. Tickets can be picked up at Will Call on the night of the event. Be sure to bring a valid form of ID when you pick up your tickets.


What is your refund policy?
All ticket sales are final. No refunds, replacements, or exchanges.


Are admission tickets to the Garden the same as tickets for Garden After Dark?
No. Garden After Dark is a special event and requires a different ticket than general admission to the Garden. Passes good for free or discounted admission to the Garden are not valid toward the purchase of Garden After Dark tickets.


What should I bring with me to the event?
Garden After Dark includes both indoor and outdoor activities so dress for the weather! Garden paths will be lit during the event, but you may also want to bring a flashlight to help guide your way.


Can I wear a costume?
Yes! Costumes are encouraged. Dress your family in their Halloween best!


Will there be food available?
Food vendors will be located in the Courtyard Garden. Stop by to find (FOOD VENDOR) Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory will also be onsite providing warm drinks and delectable sweets.


What’s included in the price of admission?
All activity stations located throughout the Garden are included with admission. The only items not included in admission are goods sold by food vendors, craft vendors, and the Red Butte Garden Gift Shop.

What activities will be available this year?
Full descriptions of this year’s activity stations can be found below.


Where do I park?
Guests are encouraged to park in the Red Butte Garden, Natural History Museum of Utah (October 19, 20, and 26 only), or the Williams building parking lots (see map below). A complimentary shuttle will run between the Garden and the Williams building to transport guests on October 21, 27, and 28. Guests are welcome to drop off passengers at the door before parking.

Don’t be late for a very important date! Join us for an enchanted evening as we journey down the rabbit hole to the whimsical, wonderful world of Wonderland. With the help of the White Rabbit, the Mad Hatter, and more, we’ll discover the large role the natural world plays in making Wonderland a magical place. From a conversation with the Caterpillar to an audience with the Queen of Hearts, we’ll explore nature’s hidden ties to the people and places of Wonderland and maybe uncover a secret or two about the natural world along the way!

With crafts, activities, light displays, and more, this is a fun-filled evening you won’t want to miss! Join us for Garden After Dark, an indoor/outdoor, costume and kid-friendly experience in the Garden to celebrate Halloween.

Thursday, Friday & Saturday nights, October 19-21 & 26-28



ACTIVITY STATIONS

Activity Stations

Prologue: Quest for the Curiosity Key

Before embarking on your adventure, pick up your family guide to Wonderland and your Curiosity Key outside the Visitor Center. The Curiosity Key is an enchanted key that unlocks the Looking Glass (a magic portal you can use to leave Wonderland and return to the real world).

Unfortunately, your key is missing its four magic gems. Complete a task for each citizen of Wonderland you encounter throughout the night and they’ll reward you with a gem to complete your key. Can you collect all four gems and return home before the night is over?

Chapter 1: Down the Rabbit Hole

Follow the White Rabbit down the rabbit hole to discover the magic entrance to Wonderland. Keep your eyes open for flying clocks, floating bookshelves, magic keys, and more as you travel from the real world to the wondrous world of Wonderland. What secrets about the natural world will you uncover along the way?

Chapter 2: Conversation with the Caterpillar

Filled with wisdom and sage advice, no trip to Wonderland is complete without a visit to the Caterpillar, Wonderland’s resident mushroom and fungi expert. Start your quest to complete your Curiosity Key by investigating some of the strange, unusual, and downright magical fungi found in the Caterpillar’s collection. Then, use what you’ve learned to decorate your own incredible shrinking mushroom charm to earn your first gem. What amazing fungi adaptations will you discover during your conversation with the Caterpillar?

Chapter 3: The Mad Hatter's Tea Party

Put on your swankiest hat and get ready to party with the Mad Hatter, Wonderland’s premiere supplier of fancy hats and herbal teas. Continue your quest by exploring the Mad Hatter’s selection of herbal teas and try to discover the magical, extraordinary way they can be used to soothe a sore throat, relieve a headache, and more. Then, play a round of Teacup Bean Bag Toss, Bowler Hat Bowling, or Flamingo Croquet to earn both your next gem and a special prize courtesy of the March Hare and the Dormouse.

Chapter 4: The Dodo's Nest

Take a break from your adventure at the Dodo’s Nest. Warm yourself by the fire cauldron and listen to a story from Ginger Parkinson, a member of the Storytellers Guild of Utah, before continuing on your journey.

Chapter 5: The Cheshire Cat's Forest of Riddles

Something is hiding in the trees of the Garden, and it looks like a large cat with a mischievous smile! Can you solve the riddles and find the clues to catch the Cheshire Cat and make it through the forest?

Chapter 6: Bill the Lizard's Living Flower Patch

You can learn an awful lot from the flowers because flowers in Wonderland talk! Stop in at Bill the Lizard’s Living Flower Patch to learn how flowers use different adaptations to attract pollinators. To earn your third gem, use what you’ve learned from Bill’s flowers to craft your own Wonderland pollinator. Then, take your pollinator on a hunt to find the six larger-than-life living flowers scattered throughout the Terrace Gardens. Can you find all six flowers?

Chapter 7: Audience with the Queen

Finish your quest at the palace of the Queen of Hearts. During your audience with the Queen, you’ll discover not only how her Wonderland roses change colors from white to red but also how some real-world roses, hydrangeas, and other plants can shapeshift and change colors, too. Earn your final gem by weaving a color-changing tassel to decorate a special bookmark crafted by the Queen of Hearts.

Chapter 8: Through the Looking Glass

Attach the final gem to your key and use it to step through the Looking Glass and return to the real world. After, tell us about your adventures and help us complete our Wonderland Mural to commemorate the your fun night. We'll thank you for joining us on our Wonderland Adventure with a special prize to continue your adventures in the natural world at world.

Eye Spy the White Rabbit

As you travel around Wonderland to complete your Curiosity Key, keep track of how many White Rabbits you find hidden throughout the Garden. At the end of the night, enter your guess to win one of six complimentary Family Memberships. How many White Rabbits can you find?

Picture Perfect Story Stops

Get your camera ready! During your quest, you may come across a few iconic scenes from Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland by Lewis Carroll. When you find one, feel free to stop and snap a picture to remember your adventure in Wonderland!

Fire Barrels

Stop and ask for directions to the next stop on your Wonderland adventure! Our friendly volunteers provide general information and advice as well as a spot to warm up before continuing on your journey.
