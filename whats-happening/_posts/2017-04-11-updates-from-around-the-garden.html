As the snow melts and the garden begins to bloom, life awakens at Red Butte. Our collections of conifers, roses, daffodils, and more are alive with the spirit of spring. The garden has never looked so good!

As our garden changes colors and forms, so has our website. We at Red Butte have worked hard to create a refreshed and streamlined way for you to access our events and information. The new site brings a modern design, enhanced functionality, and a mobile optimized experience for visitors on the go. We hope you enjoy it!

With warmer weather on our doorstep, it’s time to start planning for the 2017 Outdoor Concert Series. Tickets will be available for garden members online on Monday, April 24th at 7PM and in-person on Tuesday, April 25th. Tickets for the general public go on sale Monday, May 1st at 9AM both in-person and online. Renew or purchase a Garden-Plus Membership level or above by April 21st in order to participate in the April 24th member-only online presale.

For more information, CLICK HERE

Transform your garden by picking up some new plants at the annual Spring Plant Sale! The members-only day is May 12th while the general public day is May 13th. Here you will be able to purchase a range of indoor and outdoor plant species. Come check it out!

For more information, CLICK HERE

Finally, the new Water Conservation Garden Grand Opening is May 20th. This inspiring new space will showcase the beauty and diversity of water-wise plants. Enjoy plants of all varieties, each with unique methods of conserving water and living in a desert ecosystem.

For more information, CLICK HERE
