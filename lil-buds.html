Classes for preschool kids ages 3-4 with a parent
Wednesdays, April 4 through October 3 from 10:30AM - Noon


Registration opens Monday, February 26 at 9AM for Garden Members and Wednesday, February 28 at 9AM to the General Public.

Bring your preschooler to a pint-sized class filled with fun crafts, hands-on activities, Garden explorations, and more. Plus, each class is designed for caregivers to attend and participate with their Lil' Bud. Come make new friends, prepare for preschool, and explore the wonders of the natural world.

Lil' Buds Classes - Caregiver included in price.
Garden Members: $16 per class / General Public: $20 per class

Class price includes day admission for caregiver and child to Red Butte Garden on the day of class.

Please note, Lil' Buds classes are once a week.
