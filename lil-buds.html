Bring your preschooler to a pint-sized class with our educators in Red Butte Garden’s Lil’ Buds program. Classes feature a special exploration in the Garden, a fun craft or activity, a snack, and an engaging story connected to our age-appropriate themes. All classes are designed for caregivers to attend and participate with their child.

Lil' Buds Classes are offered once a week, every week for five weeks. Classes may be taken in the full series of five or individually. Our goal is to help your child make new friends, prepare for preschool, and develop curiosity while exploring the natural world.

Lil' Buds individual class cost per child (ages 3-4) - Caregiver included in price

Garden Members: $16 per class / General Public: $20 per class


    Registering for classes

  • Space for each class is limited to 12 Lil' Buds
  • Classes are designed for both a child and caregiver to attend
  • Class price includes day admission for caregiver and child to Red Butte Garden on the day of class
  • Classes are 90 minutes long, from 10:30AM - NOON in Em's Sprout House located in the Red Butte Garden Children's Garden
