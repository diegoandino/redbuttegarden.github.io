2020 Lil' Buds registration will open on February 24 to Garden members and February 26 to the public.

Bring your preschooler (ages 3-4) to a pint-sized class with our educators in Red Butte Garden’s Lil’ Buds program.

Lil' Buds Classes are offered once a week, every week for five weeks. Classes feature a special exploration in the Garden, a fun craft or activity, a snack, and an engaging story connected to our age-appropriate themes. All classes are designed for caregivers to attend and participate with their child.

Classes may be taken in the full series of five or individually. Our goal is to help your child make new friends, prepare for preschool, and develop curiosity while exploring the natural world.

Lil' Buds individual class cost includes one child (ages 3-4) and a parent or caregiver.

Thursdays from 10-11:30AM (90 minutes) located in the Red Butte Garden Children's Garden and Em's Sprout House.

Garden members: $16 per class / General public: $20 per class


    Registering for classes

  • Space for each class is limited to 12 Lil' Buds
  • Classes are designed for both a child and caregiver to attend
  • Class price includes day admission for caregiver and child to Red Butte Garden on the day of class
