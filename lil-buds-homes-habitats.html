LIL' BUDS CLASSES

Homes & Habitats

Day camps for preschool children (ages 3-4) and a caregiver.


Come join us, along with your lil’ bud and we’ll learn all about the animals that call Red Butte Garden home. Where do they live? What do they eat? How do they run? Why do they like living in the Garden? The answers to all these questions and more will be discovered at Lil’ Buds.
