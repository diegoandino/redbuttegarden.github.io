REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Class Times

THURSDAY AND FRIDAY, JULY 26 & 27 @ 6-9PM and SATURDAY, JULY 28 @ 8AM-4PM


In this class, you will learn sketching, journaling and quick painting techniques using a blue pencil, graphite and watercolor pencils to create colorful and detailed sketches. Sketchbook, Two evenings of creative exercises will get you started on a wonderful life-long habit. On Saturday, the 28th, we will meet at the entrances to Big Cottonwood canyon and carpool to Albion Basin at Alta ski resort to sketch and participate in beginner and intermediate wildflower walks, led by wildflower experts. This is part of the Wasatch Wildflower Festival, held at Brighton, Solitude, and Snowbird. A wonderful wildflower experience. See Wasatch Wildflower Festival, 2018.
