REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Class Times

THURSDAY AND FRIDAY, JULY 26 & 27 @ 6-9PM and SATURDAY, JULY 28 @ 8AM-4PM


In this class, you will learn sketching, journaling, and quick painting techniques using a blue pencil, graphite and watercolor pencils to create colorful and detailed sketches. Two evenings in Red Butte Garden of creative exercises will help get you started on a wonderful life-long habit. Then on Saturday July 28, we will meet near the entrance to Little and Big Cottonwood Canyons and carpool to Albion Basin at Alta Ski Resort to sketch and participate in beginner and intermediate wildflower walks led by wildflower experts. The Wasatch Wildflower Festival held at Brighton, Solitude and Snowbird will also be going on. A wonderful wildflower experience. See Wasatch Wildflower Festival, 2018.

MATERIALS LIST

Items to be provided to the Artists:


  • 5x7 wirebound sketchbook, wildflower check list, templates and color and flower detail information
  • A field kit containing watercolor and graphite pencils, eraser, sharpener, water container, watercolor brush and more
  • A portfolio of wildflower photos from Alta area and instructions for drawing various flower configurations and more
