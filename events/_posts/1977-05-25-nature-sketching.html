REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Class Times

THURSDAY AND FRIDAY, JULY 18 & 19 @ 6-9PM and SATURDAY, JULY 20 @ 9AM-4PM


In this class, you will learn sketching, journaling, and quick painting techniques using a blue pencil, graphite and watercolor pencils to create colorful and detailed sketches. This class will help get you started on a wonderful life-long habit.

MATERIALS LIST

Items to be provided to the Artists:


  • 5x7 wirebound sketchbook, wildflower check list, templates and color and flower detail information
  • A field kit containing watercolor and graphite pencils, eraser, sharpener, water container, watercolor brush and more
  • A portfolio of wildflower photos from Alta area and instructions for drawing various flower configurations and more
