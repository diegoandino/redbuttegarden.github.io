REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Class Times

THURSDAY AND FRIDAY, JULY 26 & 27 @ 6-9PM and SATURDAY, JULY 28 @ 8AM-4PM


In this class, you will learn sketching, journaling and quick painting techniques using a blue pencil, graphite and watercolor pencils to create colorful and detailed sketches. 2 evenings of creative exercises will get you started on a wonderful life long habit. On Saturday the 28th we will meet at the entrance to Little and big Cottonwood canyons and carpool to Albion basin at Alta ski resort to sketch and participate in beginner and intermediate wildflower walks led by wildflower experts. This is a part of the Wasatch Wildflower festival held at Brighton, Solitude and Snowbird. A wonderful wildflower experience. See Wasatch Wildflower Festival, 2018.

MATERIALS LIST

Items Provided to the Artist:


  • 5x7 wirebound sketchbook, wildflower check list, templates and color and flower detail information
  • A field kit containing watercolor and graphite pencils, eraser, sharpener, water container, watercolor brush and more
  • A portfolio of wildflower photos from Alta area and instructions for drawing various flower configurations and more
