Beetles of the order Coleoptera constitute the largest group of insects on earth, representing a quarter of all living organisms and a third of all animals. Come and learn why beetles are such a big success story, while creating mini beetle masterpieces. Select, draw and paint your own Utah Beetle collection using watercolor, colored pencil and graphite. Learn to create iridescence using interference watercolor.
