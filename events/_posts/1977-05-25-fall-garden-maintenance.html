Class Times

THURSDAY, OCTOBER 4 @ 6 - 9PM and SATURDAY, OCTOBER 6 @ 10AM - 1PM


REGISTRATION REQUIRED


Get a jump on spring gardening this fall. Simple garden clean-up along with dividing, transplanting, pruning, and mulching will leave you ahead of the game come spring. We'll discuss which perennials to cut back and which to leave until spring, tips for healthy fall transplanting, dividing irises and bulbs, which trees and shrubs can be pruned in fall, and how to protect your garden through mulching. Join us in the Garden as we get hands-on experience cutting back perennials, dividing, transplanting, and pruning. Co-sponsored with Lifelong Learning. Members pay $68 + sp fee, register for section 002.
