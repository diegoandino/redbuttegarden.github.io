SATURDAY, MARCH 10 @ 11AM-1PM


Curious about volunteering at Red Butte Garden?

Join us at the Volunteer Fair Open House any time between 11AM and 1PM to meet our Volunteer Crew Leads and learn more about the many opportunities for you.

Volunteer positions include: Garden Guides, Horticulture, Youth Programs, and Special Events. Bring your teenagers ages 14 and up!
