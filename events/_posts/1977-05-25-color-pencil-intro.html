Class Times

THURSDAY, AUGUST 2, FRIDAY, AUGUST 3 @ 6-9PM & SATURDAY, AUGUST 4 @ 9AM-4PM


Color pencils are a relatively new medium as they were introduced in the early 19th century and are being used today by more and more artists creating beautiful works of art. This class will provide you with valuable information and creative exercises that will inspire you to fall in love with this wonderful, portable and popular medium.


MATERIALS LIST

Materials for artist to bring to class:


  • A set of Premier Prismacolor pencils (24) in set
  • 3 Prismacolor pencils that are not included in the 24-pencil set but necessary for class exercises and projects. Can be purchased at Blick store in Sugarhouse, Michaels, and Hobby Lobby

    • Lemon Yellow #915

    • Magenta #930

    • Ultramarine #902

  • Strathmore drawing paper 300 or 400 series 9x12

  • Tracing Paper, 9 x 12 - Canson brand recommended for translucency available at Hobby Lobby. 40% coupon.

  • Scotch Brand removable tape

  • 1 Prismacolor #1077 colorless blending/burnishing pencil

Items Provided to artists:


  • 1 foam board easel

  • printed exercises

  • Portfolio of exercises and colored pencil information

  • Poster Tack for removing colored pencil
