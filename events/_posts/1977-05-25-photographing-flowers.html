REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Prerequisite: Nuts and Bolts (LLART 193), Digital Photography I (LLART 115), or take our quiz: go to www.lifelong.utah.edu or call 801-587-5433. Co-sponsored with Lifelong Learning.



SATURDAY
SEPTEMBER 17, Noon-5PM



Flowers have captivated photographers throughout history with color, texture and elegance. Learn to get the perfect shot with tips and tricks in composition, lighting, and camera set up and placement. Classroom discussion is followed by hands-on practice in the garden.

Please bring your camera and owner's manual to class and tripod if you have one. Any camera is acceptable.
