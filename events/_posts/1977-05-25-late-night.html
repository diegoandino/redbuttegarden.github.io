Get ready to explore the natural world in a whole new way at Late Night in the Garden!

Join us for an unforgettable evening of lawn games like bocce ball, ladder toss, cornhole, frisbees, and hula hooping. LEGO® brick-challenges, and crafts including mask making and butterfly suncatchers. Plus, you'll have full access to our summer exhibit Nature Connects®: Art with LEGO® Bricks.

Top it off with a late-night screening of The LEGO Movie on the Amphitheatre lawn.

Everything is awesome! Don't miss out on this fun-filled night in the Garden! Perfect for families, friends, date-night, and more!
