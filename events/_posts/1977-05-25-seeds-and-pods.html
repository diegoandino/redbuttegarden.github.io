Class Times

THURSDAY and FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 7 & 8 @ 6-9PM and SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 9 @ 9AM-4PM


Nature’s seed container, the pod and the seeds within are the focus of this course. The artists enrolled will choose the media of their choice e.g. watercolor, color pencil, graphite or pen and ink. Work will include rendering fine detail, depicting smooth, fuzzy and textural surfaces. Discovery trips into the garden will be encouraged. Art works will be done on 4x4 papers of choice and created in the media of choice.


MATERIALS LIST

  • Tracing paper pad, 9x12 or 11x14 (Canson Calique red white and Black cover) only available locally at Hobby Lobby – 40% off coupon. The very best tracing paper and the most translucent.

  • Please bring 4x4 pieces of WC paper, Bristol paper smooth finish (good for pen and Ink) and/or Drawing paper. Artists’ choice.

  • Art Tools: Brushes, fiber tipped pens, technical pens, Pen holders and nibs, color pencils, graphite pencils bring what you have, Artists’ choice.

  • Bring watercolors, inks (browns such as walnut)

  • A roll of Scotch brand removable (very important word on package) tape Purple plaid card. Office Max and Staples should have. Michaels sometimes and a roll of white ¾” Artists’ Tape available at Blick art store in Sugarhouse.

Items Provided:


  • Accordion presentation album Paste or tape in your finished art works

  • Small easels 2-3 per artist

  • 4x4 gray and tan toned drawing papers

  • Blue pencil if needed

Please bring your seeds and pods so we have a variety to swap and draw.
