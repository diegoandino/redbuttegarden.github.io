REGISTRATION REQUIRED


Experience drawing with sterling silver (optional gold or copper) on specially treated paper. This classic technique was used by Leonardo Da Vinci and produces unsurpassed incredible detail.

MATERIALS LIST

Artists should bring an enlarged photo of a favorite flower or plant, SIZE 5x7 or 5x5 to be traced and transferred to Color-aid paper I will provide. Artists can apply pastel color with Carb Othello pastel pencils(optional) if desired before rendering with silverpoint

PENCILS

  • Graphite: HB and 2H
  • Pastel pencils: I use Stabilo CarbOthello as they can easily be dry blended and smudged AND are Aquarellable making them a multi media medium. They are available locally at Blick/Utrecht art supplies in Sugarhouse as single pencils, 1.33 apiece. Buy 1-4 colors to match some of the flower colors in your photo

LEAD HOLDER

  • “clutch” lead holder which is a form of mechanical pencil. Push down on the top and little steel claws emerge from tip and grab leads or silver wire. Buy a 2mm Clutch lead holder available at Blick/Utrecht art supply in Sugarhouse for under $10 which will accomodate a 3”length of 12 gauge silver wire. Silver wire provided in class.

PAPER

  • 9X12 or larger pad or sheets of Tracing paper. “Canson” brand is the most translucent
  • White or a pastel colored“Color-aid” paper will be provided for your flower/plant artwork. Artist choice.
  • Sheets of “Color-aid” paper can be purchased at www.coloraid.com. “Color-aid” paper is one of the best surfaces for silverpoint and the paper is not available in Salt Lake City. Small sample packs are available but not full sheets.

We will also explore other surfaces using silverpoint “ground” which can be brushed onto other surfaces..

OTHER

  • Scotch “Removable” magic tape (in blue plaid holder) available in office supply stores.
  • A kneaded eraser
  • Q-tips and/or blending stumps also known tortillions
  • Ruler
  • X-acto Knife
  • Metal emery board used for filing ones nails. Use: filing the tip of silver wire.

I will provide transfer paper and silver wire at no cost and color-aide paper for 1st project and there will be sizes and colors of color-aid paper for purchase in class.
