Class Times

FRIDAY, APRIL 28 @ 11:30AM -1 2:30PM


REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Celebrate Arbor Day at the Garden! Stop by for a lunchtime tour with Crystal Kim, Garden Horticulturist, to explore deciduous trees in the Garden. Discover unique trees that are good candidates for your own yard and how they can lower your energy bill year round.

Bring your questions and leave with a free tree to take home due to the generous support of Rocky Mountain Power.

Tour meets in the Courtyard Garden. Space limited to 20 guests.
