Guest Lecturer: Rachel Taylor, Research Associate for the Southwest Monarch Study

THURSDAY, MAY 14

6:30-7:30PM


Where have all the monarch butterflies gone? Why should we care? Join us to learn about the declining number of monarchs worldwide, their dramatic decline west of the Rockies, and what we can do to save them. Discover how to find monarch activity on milkweed (eggs, caterpillars and chrysalis), how and where to plant milkweed, and how to get involved in monarch conservation. Seating is limited to 60 people.
