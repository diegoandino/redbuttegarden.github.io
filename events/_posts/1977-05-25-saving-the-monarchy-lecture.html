Guest Lecturer: Rachel Taylor, Citizen Scientist

THURSDAY, MAY 14

6:30-7:30PM


Where have all the monarchs gone and why should we care? Learn about local efforts to save the monarch butterflies through finding milkweed (and eggs, caterpillars and chrysalis), planting and caring for milkweed, and tagging butterflies for migration.
