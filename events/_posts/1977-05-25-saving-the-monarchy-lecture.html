Guest Lecturer: Rachel Taylor, Citizen Scientist

THURSDAY, MAY 14

6:30-7:30PM


Where have all the monarchs gone? Why should we care? Learn about the declining numbers of monarch butterflies worldwide, and local efforts to save them. Learn how to find milkweed (and eggs, caterpillars and chrysalis), how to plant and care for milkweed, and how to get involved with tagging butterflies for migration. Limited to 60 people.
