TUESDAYS, APRIL 24 - MAY 1
6PM - 8PM

REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Want to save water in your landscape but not sure how to begin? Join us as we look at plant varieties and landscaping techniques that help conserve water and make your yard the envy of the neighborhood. Learn which water-wise perennials to combine for continual bloom; how to group plants according to water needs, color, and texture; and the unique design and maintenance requirements of water-wise gardening. Co-sponsored with Lifelong Learning.
