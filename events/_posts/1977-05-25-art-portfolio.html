REGISTRATION REQUIRED


In this hands-on class, students will design and build a portfolio to hold 11 x 14 inch artwork or any papers of that size. The portfolio will protect these items from loss or damage. Students will learn bookbinding skills and techniques necessary to construct the portfolio.



Artist Bio:

BS Biology/Botany with Teaching Credential, Cal Poly Pomona
BFA Painting and Drawing, University of Utah
Instructor of Calligraphy, Freelance Calligrapher


PROVIDED MATERIALS

  • 11 3/8 X 14 ¾ inch mat board
  • Book cloth
  • Decorative Paper
  • Templates, gages and guides
  • Liner paper (stonehenge)
  • Mechanical pencil
  • Hammers., dividers, and wedges for students use
  • Glue Brush
  • Bricks for weights
  • Metal Edge Ruler

MATERIALS TO BE OBTAINED BY STUDENT

Resource: Dick Blick Store 1025 East 2100 South Salt Lake City, Utah

  • X-acto knife and #11 blades
  • Cutting mat or cardboard to cut on
  • P V A glue (Neutral PH adhesive) 8 oz / Dick Blick Store
  • Decorative paper and one yard of gross grain ½ inch ribbon to match (optional)
  • 45 degree plastic triangle 3 ½ “ see through plastic
  • Bone Folder / Dick Blick Store
  • Scissors

SYLLABUS

Wed. 6 -9

1. Cut boards to square.

2. Cut and glue liner paper to cloth, Stack to dry.

3. Mark measurements on boards

4. Cut liner papers

Thurs. 6 – 9

1. Cut and glue spine pieces.

2. Make corner gage template

3. Cut decorative paper.

Saturday 9 - 4

1. Glue decorative paper to boards.

2. Cut and insert ribbon

3. Score and glue in flaps

4. Line Portfolio
