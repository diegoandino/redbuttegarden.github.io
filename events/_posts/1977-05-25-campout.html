Have you ever wondered what happens at the Garden when the last guest leaves? The only way to find out is at one of our campouts! Bring your tent, camping gear, and a picnic dinner to enjoy an overnight, low-stress camping experience complete with flushing toilets and running water. Spend the evening relaxing at your campsite or join us for the bevy of interactive family adventures we have planned including games, crafts, s’mores, and more. We’ll also provide a scrumptious, catered breakfast by Waffle Love Saturday morning to complete your camping adventure. You haven’t camped until you’ve camped Red Butte Garden style!

Online registration only. All ages welcome. Registration required for all participants.

Friday night dinner, tents, and other camping gear NOT provided, please plan to bring your own.



FRIDAY, JUNE 21 @ 5:30PM – SATURDAY, JUNE 22, 8:30AM

Registration opens Monday, June 3 at 9AM.
