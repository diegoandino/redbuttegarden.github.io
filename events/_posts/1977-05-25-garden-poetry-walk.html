Did you know April is National Poetry Month? Celebrate this unique literary art with a stroll through the Garden to find eleven poetry boxes featuring poems written by winners of our Spring Poetry Contest.

Throughout the month, you can also interact with our winning poets via your cell phone on a special audio tour featuring recordings of our contest winners performing readings of their poems. The audio tour is free, but normal cell phone carrier usage rates apply.

To access the audio tour, please follow the instructions posted at each poetry box. While enjoying the tour, please be considerate of other Garden guests and listen to audio content through headphones or your phoneâ€™s earpiece.


The winners of our 2018 Poetry Month Poetry Contest are:

  • 1. I, Spring by Taisha Ostler
  • 2. Waking Up by Ella Housen, Youth Winner
  • 3. Spring Fever by Katharine Goodman
  • 4. The Sunrise Hour by Janna Pate
  • 5. Sometimes in February by Marilyn Bushman-Carlton
  • 6. The Dawn of Spring by Sophie Sutherland, Youth Winner
  • 7. Waiting by Mary Greenwood
  • 8. Spring by Terresa Wellborn
  • 9. Renewal by Barbara Smith
  • 10. It is Spring! It is Spring! by Jolene Whitney
  • 11. Simply Spring by Jace Mensior, Youth Winner
