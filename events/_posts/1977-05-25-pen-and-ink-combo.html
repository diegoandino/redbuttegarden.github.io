REGISTRATION FOR THIS CLASS IS CLOSED


This is a combined Pen & Ink 1 & 2 class. Artists will use traditional nibs and holders, as well as fiber-tipped pens and learn to use the ruling pen. Artists will complete several projects - creative and scientific – in preparation for scientific illustration classes.

MATERIALS LIST

Dinky Dips, (ink containers and ink), Pen nibs and holders provided for each artist

  • A pad of tracing paper (Canson Calique red white and Black cover) only available locally at Hobby Lobby – 40% off coupon. The very best tracing paper and the most translucent
  • A roll of Scotch brand removable (very important word on package) tape Purple plaid card. Office Max and Staples should have. Michaels sometimes and a roll of white ¾” Artists’ Tape available at Blick art store in Sugarhouse
  • A 12”ruler
  • A ruling pen, Google up “ruling pen” to see photos.. This is an optional tool. In past classes the quality of Alvin, I recommended has deteriorated dramatically as the points do not line up well. I will research suppliers. You might already have one that came in a drafting set
  • 3-4 Fiber tipped drawing pens. Recommended brands that can be purchased at Blick/Utrecht in Sugarhouse. Staedler Pigment Liner Best, archival, waterproof, lightfast and Longer lasting. Best buy is a set of 4 pens in case, fine line to medium thick. Price: 13.15 locally. You can also buy individual pens 3.59 to add to your collection. Cheaper online
  • 3-4 Micron Pigma archival, waterproof, fade proof and is also available at Blick Utrecht, but is not as long lasting but are my 2nd choice because they too are available locally, sets available. Buy 005, .01, .02 and .05. Or Set Of 3 Point Sizes, Black — One each of 01 (0.25 mm), 03 (0.35 mm), and 05 (0.45 mm). Black ink. ADD the 005 which is not included in set price of 6.59. Individual pens cost: 2.59
  • 1. Pencils: 2H,4H and HB and Pentel Clic Eraser a white plastic retractable eraser sold at Staples and Office Max and a kneaded gray eraser wildly available.
  • 11x14 Bristol 300 series “Smooth” paper pad. You might already have this paper. If not: Strathmore 300 series is a good Bristol pad. Smooth or Plate finish.
