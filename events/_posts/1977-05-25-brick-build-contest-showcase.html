CONTEST DEADLINE MAY 21 / HOW TO ENTER

    HOW TO ENTER

    Complete the CONTEST ENTRY FORM and submit a photo of your sculpture by May 21, 2018. Images will be shared throughout the contest on the Garden's Facebook and Instagram.

    Remember, the subject of your sculpture must be related to the natural world or Red Butte Garden.

    Entries are due by May 21, 2018 at 11:59PM to be eligible for judging. There is no charge to enter your sculpture into the contest.


    JUDGING CRITERIA

    Contest entries will be judged on: Building Skill, Creativity, Originality, Theme Interpretation, Presentation

    A panel of Red Butte Garden staff will judge the first round of photo submissions. The public will vote and select the grand prize winners on June 2, 2018 at the Showcase.


    JUNE 2 FINAL ROUND SHOWCASE

    The top three entries from each age division must be delivered to the Red Butte Garden Visitor Center on Saturday, June 2 between 8AM and 10AM. Finalists will be notified a week prior to the Showcase if their sculpture has been chosen to compete.


    AGE DIVISIONS

    YOUTH (age 17 & under): Ages 7 & under / Ages 8-12 / Ages 13-17

    ADULT (age 18 & up): Amateur / Professional



CONTEST RULES

Full Contest Rules

  • Registration is free and open to builders of all ages.
  • Images of sculptures must be submitted by May 21, 2018 to be considered for preliminary judging. Only sculptures chosen as finalists from the online portion of the contest will be displayed at the Garden on June 2, 2018. Drop-in sculptures may NOT be submitted to the Garden for the showcase and finals.
  • Sculptures must be built on a base no larger than 24 by 24 inches. The base can be wood, cardboard, a LEGO brick flat base, etc. and can be decorated as desired. Completed sculptures may be no taller than four feet tall.
  • Sculptures chosen for the Showcase must be brought ready to be displayed, secured with no loose parts. Construction will not be permitted on site.
  • Original LEGO® brick designs only. Sculptures made from kits will be disqualified. Only nature-themed sculptures or sculptures related to Red Butte Garden will be considered for competition.
  • All builders agree to have their sculptures on display from 10AM to 3PM on Saturday, June 2, 2018, should their sculpture be chosen as a finalist. Finalists' sculptures must be picked up by 5PM on Monday, June 4. Sculptures not retrieved by this time will become property of Red Butte Garden.
  • Red Butte Garden is not responsible for damage to sculptures.
  • All finalists whose sculptures are chosen for the Showcase will receive TWO complimentary passes to Red Butte Garden to use on the day of the Showcase, June 2, 2018 (one pass for the builder and one pass for a chaperone or guest). All other guests will be required to pay admission.
  • Red Butte Garden retains the right to refuse entry to any sculpture it deems inappropriate for display.
  • Red Butte Garden staff members, volunteers, and their families may enter their sculptures for sharing on social media but are not eligible for judging or prizes.

  • Do I need to stay at the Garden for the entire day during the Showcase in June 2?
    • No, but contestants are welcome to spend the day at the Garden to enjoy the Nature Connects exhibit.
  • Is there a size limit?
    • Your creation must be on a base no larger than 24 by 24 inches. The base can be wood, cardboard, a LEGO flat base, etc. and can be decorated to fit with your project. Your completed project may be no taller than four feet tall.
  • Is there a theme for my creation?
    • Yes! Sculptures should reflect the natural world or Red Butte Garden to be eligible for competition.
  • What materials may I use?
    • Use your own LEGO®, DUPLO®, Mega Bloks® or other LEGO compatible building bricks.
  • Are group-entries allowed?
    • No, this competition is designed for individuals.
  • Can I build my sculpture on-site?
    • No, you should create your sculpture off-site, and submit a photo with your completed CONTEST ENTRY FORM for first-round judging. Only sculptures that have been chosen from the online portion of the competition will allowed to be displayed at the Garden.
  • Do I have to display my sculpture at the Garden?
    • Yes, in order to compete in the final Showcase your sculpture must be displayed at the Garden on June 2, 2018 for judging by the public.
    • If your sculpture wins First Place in any age division, you have the option to display your sculpture at the Garden throughout the Nature Connects exhibit, June 2 - Sept 16, or to take it home.
