Please submit your Performer Application on or before March 31, 2018.

Performer Guidelines & Performance Notes
  • Groups perform once from 6:30 to 7:30 PM
  • Each group is paid $750 for their participation
  • Performers are encouraged to interact with the audience following their performance (answer questions, demonstarte dance moves, etc.)
  • Audiences range from 300 to 500 guests
  • Performances typically occure outdoors in the Garden, but a backup space is reserved indoors in case of inclement weather
  • A current W9 form is required with each application.
  • If selected to perform, each group (or a representative from the group) will be required to tour the performance site and sign a University approved performer agreement.

Groups selected to perform at Monday Family Night will be notified by the end of April 2018.
