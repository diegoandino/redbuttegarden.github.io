Interested in performing at Monday Family Night?

We're searching for Utah's best performing groups who are interested in performing at Red Butte Garden this summer. If your group is local, offers unique entertainment, and an engaging performance, we'd love to learn more about you! Check below for more details and submit your application today.


Click above to submit your Performer Application on or before March, 31, 2019 to be considered for our 2019 season.


Performer Guidelines & Performance Notes
  • Groups perform once from 6:30 to 7:30 PM
  • Each group is paid $750 for their participation
  • Performers are encouraged to interact with the audience following their performance (answer questions, demonstrate dance moves, etc.)
  • Audiences range from 300 to 500 guests
  • Performances typically occur outdoors in the Garden, but a backup space is reserved indoors in case of inclement weather
  • A current W9 form is required with each application.
  • If selected to perform, each group (or a representative from the group) will be required to tour the performance site and sign a University approved performer agreement.

Groups selected to perform at Monday Family Night will be notified by the end of April 2019.
