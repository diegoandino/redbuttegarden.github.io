Terry Scopes is an abstract expressionist artist who works primarily in acrylic. Her use of vibrant colors and bold marks is an unusual interpretation of realism and fantasy.

Ms. Scopes studied art at the University of Utah, as well as with extraordinary painters that includes Nicholas Wilton, Lynn Whipple and Flora Bowley. She currently lives and works out of her home studio in Salt Lake City, Utah. Her work has been exhibited throughout the West and can be found in private collections in the United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia.

For more please visit: https://terryscopes.com/gallery-2-2/
