Spend a morning at Red Butte Garden creating a beautiful, living, succulent wreath. These versatile wreaths can grace your garden, become the dramatic center-piece that sets the mood for a special dinner, or a holiday accent for a door or a mantel. We'll provide a 13-inch wreath frame, moss and succulent plants, as well as cover care and maintenance of your living wreath.
