Red Butte Garden & The Adopt-A-Native-Elder Program Present:
THE WEAVERS' STORIES



This will be a great opportunity to:

  • Listen to fascinating stories, as Native American Weavers tell about their rugs
  • Gain knowledge of the spiritual significance of corn in many Native American cultures and how it is used in ceremonies
  • Learn about the current conditions facing Traditional Navajo Elders

On-site demonstrations include:

  • Traditional Navajo Dancers perform from 5:30-6PM - right before the event
  • Weaving Demonstrations: Weaving Ancient Designs & Spider Woman's influence
  • Explore the ethno-botanical ties between the Native American healing plants and many plants found in Red Butte Garden
  • Corn grinding demonstration


Traditional Navajo rugs and jewelry will be displayed and presented for sale.
100% of the proceeds from rug sales go directly to the weavers.
Free admission for the event from 6-8PM
