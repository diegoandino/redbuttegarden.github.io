REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Class Times

FRIDAYS, APRIL 5 - MAY 3 @ 6 - 9PM


Tree Portraits of Red Butte Garden-2 (Spring) is a continuation of Tree Portraits of Red Butte Garden-1 (Winter) and is open for new registrations. Artists will revisit the 5 selected trees of the first class and will sketch and photograph Leafing and blooming or vice versa. In the classroom we will continue to add detailed pages to our tree portrait album. Make up classes for Tree Portraits -1 (Winter) will be offered after Tree Portraits-2 and before Tree Portraits-3, which is scheduled for Mondays: July 29-August 26, 6-9PM. Details: email or call Elaine Hultgren: ehult@cox.net or 801-502-4700 and leave a message.

PROVIDED TO NEW STUDENTS

  • Portrait album
  • Binder with valuable tree information

    • MATERIALS LIST - ARTIST/STUDENT TO PROVIDE

    I will be doing step-by-step demonstrations using graphite, watercolor and colored pencil. If you love working in another medium, please feel free to bring them to class.

    • Pencils: 4H, 3H, 2H, HB, 2B, 4B. A Col-Erase labeled Blue #20044 pencil, available at Blick Sugarhouse or online at Dick Blick
    • Your collection of watercolors and/or colored pencils
    • A Pentel “Clic” white pen eraser, available at Office supply stores and Wal-Mart
    • Paper: 2 packs of Strathmore Redi-Cut Hot Press 5x7 25 sheets per pack Total 50 sheets. You will need 40 sheets, 8 per tree. Available at Dick Blick and Jerry's Art-A-Rama for $6.99 per pack
    • 5x7 Strathmore Redi Cut Watercolor paper pack of 25. 2 might be good to have for trial and error.
    • 9x12 tracing paper. Suggested Canson red and Black cover at Hobby Lobby. or Daler Rowney tracing paper comparable to Canson and cheaper. Was available at Wal-mart for around $3.00
    • Tools: Ruler, 6" or 12"
    • Sketchbook or pad to sketch onsite if it is warm enough
    • Labeled Removable tape, available at Office Max, maybe Michaels
    • Labeled white Artist's Tape
    • A camera, either on your phone or tablet, or regular camera
