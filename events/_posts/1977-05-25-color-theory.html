REGISTRATION REQUIRED


Explore color through a series of exercises that will familiarize the student with this complex subject. The history of color and color wheels, color vocabulary, color harmony, and colors in nature will be studied. Using a limited palette, students will create color wheels and several charts using colored pencils.



MATERIALS LIST

Art supplies to bring to class

  • Colored pencils, Prisma-Color Premier:
    • A set of 24 Prisma-color Premier pencils contain all but 3 pencils needed to do color wheels (Primary cool and warm,Secondary,Tertiary, & Value) and other exercises for this class.
    • The 4 that you need to buy if you do not have:
      • #902 Ultramarine
      • #903 True Blue
      • #915 Lemon yellow
      • #931 Dark Purple
    • NOTE: If you have a different brand but at least a set of 24 please bring and we can color match

  • Please bring any completed color wheels from other classes e.g. Complement wheel for Colored Pencil - 1 class

  • Erasers:
    • Kneaded gray eraser
    • “Pentel Clic Eraser” a retractable eraser with a plastic pen-like holder available at Office supply stores (Wal-Mart has them for $1.55)

  • Artist Tape: 3/4" or 1/2” white tape sold at art supply stores

  • “Scotch Brand” Removable Tape: Sold at office supply stores

  • X-acto knife: If you own one
