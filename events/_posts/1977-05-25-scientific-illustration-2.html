REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Artists will continue with Scientific Illustration-2 by constructing a voucher or plant sheet using a live plant to sketch and draw the habit and a fruit with seeds and flower to dissect, draw, label and measure. All plant parts will be drawn on tracing paper, transferred to Brisrol and inked and cut out and arranged on the 11x17 sheets from the NHMU Herbarium, you will take finished sheet to office max and have copies made on copier using 11x17 size paper. we are unable to freeze dry the plant parts we will work from. This will be an interesting and valuable experience of composing a scientific sheet or voucher with a more hands on experience with plant parts to work from.



Three Day Workshop

FRIDAY, MAY 17, 6-9PM

MATERIALS LIST


Materials for artist to bring to class:

  • Bring a dissecting kit you received in Mary Jo’s Botany class or one you may already have. If you do not have one I will be bringing mine to share tools.
  • A pad of 11x14 of Canson tracing paper in a Red White and Black cover. (Hobby Lobby)
  • A roll of tape labeled “Removable” or “Wall Safe” A roll of white tape labeled “Artists” tape
  • Bring your fiber-tipped drawing pens and a Micron 005 pen as it makes a very fine line
  • Pencils: HB,2H,4H
  • A “Clic” eraser pen and a kneaded gray eraser (Office Stores, Blick SH, Michaels, Hobby Lobby)
  • Bristol paper, bring what you have any size
  • Sallys graphite Transfer paper if you have. I will bring what I have. To buy (Blick SH)
