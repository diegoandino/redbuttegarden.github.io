REGISTRATION REQUIRED

This workshop has a focus of fins, feathers and fur. All pets and favorite animals are welcome as a photographic image. Please bring to class a sharp COLOR 9x12 or 8x10 reference photo. Using colored pencils, you will render your portrait using layering techniques and detailed textures to bring life to your subject. Your portrait will be a lasting tribute to a beloved pet, pets or animal of your choosing. Colored pencil experience preferred.



Three Day Workshop

THURSDAY, JANUARY 31, 6-9PM
FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 1, 6-9PM
SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 2, 9AM-4PM



MATERIALS LIST

Materials for artist to bring to class:

  • A set of Prismacolor pencils, 24 or more, plus 3 not in the set of 24, as follows:

    • Lemon Yellow #915

    • Magenta #930

    • Ultramarine #902

  • Prismacolor Verithin pencils if you have them
  • Foam board easel if you have one from another class (if not, I will have some available.)
  • Tracing Paper (Canson)
  • Scotch Brand removable tape
  • Kneaded eraser and white Pentel "Clic" eraser
  • Hand-held pencil sharpener
  • Prismacolor #1077 colorless blending pencil
  • Col-Erase blue pencil #20044
  • 6" ruler
  • Color wheel (can be the one you made in color theory or Colored Pencil 1)
  • Graphite pencils 4H,2H, HB, 2B, and 4B
  • Pad of 9"x12" drawing paper such as Strathmore, Canson or Bee Paper
