Event Times

SATURDAY & SUNDAY, DECEMBER 1 & 2, 10AM - 5PM



Free Garden Admission Dec 1 & 2 thanks to Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts & Parks (ZAP)

Find Unique Handmade Gifts for the Holidays! 2018 marks the 18th Anniversary of the Holiday Open House & Art Fair at Red Butte Garden! The Orangerie will be filled with 19 local artists who will bring an array of handmade gifts for sale including jewelry, glass art, pottery, fiber art, and more.

We also offer complimentary cider and hot chocolate, and winter wonderland of a garden to explore. So bring your shopping list, your winter boots, and enjoy the season with us at the Garden!

And to make your shopping weekend even brigher, save 10% on all purchases in the Garden Gift Shop. Garden Members receive an additional 10% off - December 1 & 2 only at the Red Butte Garden Gift Shop Annual Holiday Sale

Call for Artist Entries - Deadline is October 12!

2018 Holiday Open House Artist Application

If you would like to vend your original, locally made, handcrafted art at the 2018 Holiday Open House please fill out our artist application and submit by Friday, October 12.
