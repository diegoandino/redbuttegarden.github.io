As Utah’s very own arboretum, there’s no better place than Red Butte Garden to celebrate Arbor Day! Visit the Garden to explore the wonderful world of trees through lectures, tours, scavenger hunts, and more "tree-rific" fun. And don't forget to pick up your FREE tree seedling courtesy of Rocky Mountain Power (while supplies last).

Thanks to funding in part by Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts, and Parks Program (ZAP) Arbor Day is a ZAP Free Day!
Enjoy free Garden admission all day.
