As Utah’s very own arboretum, there’s no place better than Red Butte Garden to celebrate Arbor Day! Visit the Garden for a day filled with "tree-rific" fun as you discover the wonderful world of trees. Don't forget to pick up your FREE tree seedling courtesy of Rocky Mountain Power (while supplies last).

Thanks to funding in part by Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts, and Parks Program (ZAP) Arbor Day is a ZAP Free Day!
Enjoy free Garden admission all day.


Things to do on Arbor Day:


Tree Giveaway - Red Butte Garden Visitor Center, All Day

Thanks to the generous support of Rocky Mountain Power, all guests leave with a free Serviceberry Tree, Amelanchier lamarckii, to take home. Trees available while supplies last!



Drop-In Activities - The Sprout House, 10 AM - 3 PM

Explore the world of trees with a trio of self-guided activites perfect for all ages, including a “tree-rific” craft to commerorate your Arbor Day celebration.



Tree Hunt & Exploration Stops - Throughout the Garden, All Day

Red Butte Garden has an amazing collection of trees! See if you can find the ten trees we’ve highlighted with signs as you explore the Garden. Along the way, look for the Exploration Stops where you can use your senses to discover the vital role leaves, trunks, roots, and seeds play in the life of a tree.



Poster Contest Ceremony - Orangerie, 10-10:45AM

Students across the state have been busy learning about trees and creating their own posters to illustrate this year’s theme, "Trees are Terrific for a Healthy Environment!” Help us recognize this year’s contest winners at the official Utah State Arbor Day Ceremony.



Tree Planting - Location the Four Seasons Garden, 10:45-11AM

Participate in our 2019 Arbor Day tree planting! A new tree will be added to the Garden’s collection to commemorate this important holiday.



Special thanks to Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts and Parks (ZAP) Program.
