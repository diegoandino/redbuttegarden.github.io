As Utah’s very own arboretum, there’s no place better than Red Butte Garden to celebrate Arbor Day. Visit the Garden for a day filled with "tree-rific" fun as you discover the wonderful world of trees. Don't forget to pick up your FREE tree seedling courtesy of Rocky Mountain Power (while supplies last).

Thanks to funding in part by Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts, and Parks Program (ZAP) Arbor Day is a ZAP Free Day.
Enjoy free Garden admission all day.


ARBOR DAY CELEBRATION ACTIVITIES



Tree Giveaway

Visitor Center || All Day

Pickup a free Serviceberry (Amelanchier lamarckii) tree seedling to take home courtesy of Rocky Mountain Power. Seedlings available while supplies last.



Drop-In Activities

The Sprout House || 10 AM - 3 PM

Explore the world of trees with a trio of self-guided activites perfect for all ages, including a “tree-rific” craft.



Poster Contest Ceremony

Orangerie || 10 - 10:45 AM

Students across the state have been busy learning about trees and creating their own posters to illustrate this year’s theme, "Trees are Terrific for a Healthy Environment!” Help us recognize this year’s contest winners at the official Utah State Arbor Day Ceremony.



Tree Planting

The Four Seasons Garden || 10:45-11AM

Visit the Four Seasons Garden to participate in our 2019 Arbor Day tree planting. A new tree will be added to the Garden’s collection to commemorate this important holiday.



Tree Hunt & Exploration Stops

Throughout the Garden || All Day

Red Butte Garden has an amazing collection of trees. See if you can find the ten trees we’ve highlighted with signs as you explore the Garden. Along the way, look for the Exploration Stops where you can use your senses to discover the vital role leaves, trunks, roots, and seeds play in the life of a tree.

Can you find these trees in the Garden?
1. Magnolia, Magnolia x loebneri ‘Leornard Messel’ | Courtyard
2. Saskatoon Serviceberry, Amelanchier alnifolia | Floral Walk
3. Paperbark Maple, Acer griseum | Floral Walk
4. Gambel Oak, Quercus gambelii | Floral Walk
5. Tulip Tree, Liriodendron tulipifera | Water Pavilion Garden
6. Bur Oak, Quercus macrocarpa | Water Conservation Garden
7. Gold Rush Dawn Redwood, Metasequoia glyptostroboides | Four Seasons Garden
8. Red Horse Chestnut, Aesculus x carnea | Children's Garden
9. Pinyon Pine, Pinus edulis | Outside Fragrance Garden
10. London Plane Tree, Platanus x acerifolia | Medicinal Garden



Tree Selfies

Throughout the Garden || All Day

Which tree is your favorite? Snap a selfie by one of the trees in our Tree Hunt (see above) and post it on Instagram using #redbuttegarden for a chance to win a great prize. Visit @redbuttegarden on Instagram for more details.
