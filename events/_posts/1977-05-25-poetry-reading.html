Celebrate spring and go on a walk through the Garden with the winners of our spring poetry contest! Throughout the month of April the Garden is hosting 11 poetry boxes, showcasing works from local poets in honor of National Poetry Month. Stroll the Garden with the poets to hear live readings and gain details about the meaning behind their words.

Authors in attendance will include:

  • Megan Olsen
  • Maya Johnson
  • Melinda Birrell
  • Sara Bybee
  • Tina Thomas
  • Pam Tucker
  • Joyce Marder
  • Christina Elizabeth
  • Carol Anderson
  • Julie Chytraus
  • Acacia Ott
