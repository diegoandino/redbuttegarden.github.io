Kick-off National Poetry Month with the winners of our 5th Annual Spring Poetry Contest at a special reception held in their honor! Mix and mingle with our poets, celebrate their winning poetry, and enjoy live readings of their poems. After, enjoy a stroll through the Garden to see the winning poems on display along the Floral Walk.

Check back in mid-March to see which of our winning poets will be in attendance!

And don't forget to visit the Garden to see all of the winning poems on display as part of our Garden Poetry Walk during the month of April to celebrate National Poetry Month!
