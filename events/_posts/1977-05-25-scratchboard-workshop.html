Scratchboard or scraperboard (UK) is the perfect medium for obtaining very fine detail. A contour line drawing is 1st drawn on paper and then transferred using white or a light color transfer paper onto the black scratchboard surface Using special sharp tools, the image is etched into the board giving the look of a very fine white line drawing not unlike a beautiful woodcut. Learn all about the tools and techniques of this unique art form and you will complete a stunning piece of artwork.
