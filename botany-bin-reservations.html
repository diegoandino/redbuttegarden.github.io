To Reserve a Botany Bin

  1. Each teacher may check out one bin for a two and one-half week time period. For extended reservations please contact us by email.
  2. Select the Bin you would like to reserve above, and then chose a date from the drop-down calendar. Next, add to your shopping cart, complete the forms and don't forget to checkout!
  3. You will receive an email confirming your approved pick-up date.
    PLEASE NOTE: Your dates are not confirmed until you receive the confirmation email.
Pick-up and drop-off hours change with Red Butte Garden's hours of operation

October 1 - April 30: 9:30AM - 5:00PM

May 1 - September 30: 9:30AM - 7:00PM
