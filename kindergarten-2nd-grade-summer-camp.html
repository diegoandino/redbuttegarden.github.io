Theater in the Garden

JULY 15 - 19
9AM - NOON
GRADE: Kindergarten - Second
AGES: 5 - 8
SESSION: JULY 15 - 19
Monday - Friday
9AM - NOON

Calling all actors and actresses! Spend the week exploring drama, dance, visual arts, and more in this theater camp. Throughout the week, you'll learn to act from a drama teacher, create props and backdrops with a stagecraft instructor, and work with your fellow thespians to produce and star in a simple, one-act play. End the week with a live performance of your play in the Garden showcasing all you've learned.
