LIL' BUDS CLASSES

How Does Your Garden Grow?

Day camps for preschool children (ages 3-4) and a caregiver.


Dig into the basics of botany and learn about the different parts of plants including roots, stems, leaves, flowers, and seeds. We’ll explore the world of plants with perfectly suited pint-sized activities like plant-inspired art projects, fun games, special plant-based snacks, botanical picture books, and more.
