Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah
JUNE 25 - 29
9AM - 4PM
GRADE: First Grade
AGES: Ages 6 - 7
SESSION 1: JUNE 25 - 29
SESSION 2: JULY 30 - AUGUST 3
Monday - Friday
9AM - 4PM

Partner Camp with Natural History Muesum of Utah (NHMU)

Explore the natural world with Red Butte Garden and the Natural History Museum of Utah! Spend mornings at Red Butte Garden looking at the natural world from the perspective of ants, raccoons, rocks, and more to discover the unique perspective each viewpoint gives you. In the afternoon travel to NHMU where you’ll investigate the natural processes surrounding you, including the changing seasons, the rock cycle, the water cycle, and more.

Drop off at Red Butte Garden by 9AM and pick up at NHMU by 4PM.

Caregivers: Please note the curriculum for Session 1 & 2 is the same.

NOTE: Registration for this class is done through NHMU


LOCATION:

Red Butte Garden / Natural History Museum of Utah - 301 Wakara Way, SLC, UT 84108

Members: $252, Public: $280
