Partner Camp with Tanner Dance

Get ready to explore the world of plants with Tanner Dance and Red Butte Garden! Spend your mornings at the Garden digging, planting, watering, and weeding your way through the plant kingdom as you learn what plants need to grow, plant your own patio container garden, and more. In the afternoon, you’ll work with Tanner Dance to learn more about plants and the connections they form through visual art, drama, and dance. You’ll end the week with a special dance performance at Tanner Dance showcasing all you’ve learned. Transportation between sites provided.

Drop off at RBG by 9AM and pick up at Tanner Dance by 4PM
