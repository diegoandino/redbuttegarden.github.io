Partner Camp with Play-Well TEKnologies

Bug lovers unite! Discover the Garden’s tiniest inhabitants as you explore the world of bugs. In the morning build and program robots in this introductory Robotics class using the LEGO® WeDo® system. Learn basic programming skills, simple engineering concepts, and the names of robot components all while working in small groups to complete projects using tilt and motion sensors. In the afternoons, you’ll learn about life cycles, social insects, water bugs, and more as you hike the Garden trails, create bug-related crafts, and conduct cool experiments. By the end of the week, you’ll be an amateur bug expert!

Partner Camp with Kidding Around Yoga

Shinrin-yoku means “to take in the forest atmosphere” or “forest bathing.” Come join us on this nature-loving adventure in the Garden! We will be taught classes from Kidding Around Yoga with Sarah Bly. Sarah is a certified kids yoga teacher and will be doing all kinds of breathing techniques, yoga poses, games and more. The rest of camp you will explore the Garden through hikes, crafts, games, and more. By the end of the week, not only will you have a greater appreciation of nature and the world around us, but you’ll also finish camp more relaxed and at one with the universe. Namaste.
