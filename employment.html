ASSISTANT LANDSCAPE DESIGNER

Administration Department
Full time, benefitted position

Assistant Landscape Designer

Administration Department
Full time, benefitted position

Salary

$12.02 - $19.00 an hour

Schedule

40 hours per week. Monday - Friday; 8:30 am to 5 pm; occasional evening and weekend

Job Summary

Red Butte Garden is looking for a Assistant Landscape Designer to work with the Landscape Architect in multiple aspects of Red Butte Garden projects, including product and construction research, materials takeoffs, quote requests from vendors, cost estimating, design plans and detailing, graphic presentations, bid documents, and construction observation.

This is a full time benefited position with funding for two years, may be extended depended on continued support.

Responsibilities

  • 1. Assists Landscape Architect and Red Butte Garden personnel as directed in developing and executing planning, design and construction project requirements.
  • 2. Prepares visual simulations and graphic presentations of proposed improvements and construction.
  • 3. Prepares draft and final graphic art documents for production of signs and plaques.
  • 4. Provides research of construction methods, materials and costs.
  • 5. Performs material takeoffs, makes quote requests and prepares labor and material estimates.
  • 6. Assists in organization and maintenance of project files.
  • 7. Integrates data from multiple drawing/graphic formats (pdf, dwg, jpg) to create plans and maps of Garden infrastructure.
  • 8. Develops construction plans and details for contractor estimates and implementation on small landscape projects throughout Red Butte Garden.
  • 9. Assists in review of plans and specifications for construction projects.
  • 10. Assists with construction observation and inspections.

Qualifications

Associate’s degree in Architectural Drafting or equivalency; one year of previous architectural drafting experience using computer aided design techniques; a working knowledge of building construction and remodeling methods and materials; and excellent written communication skills, oral communication skills and interpersonal skills required. Demonstrated customer service skills to deal effectively with others are also required.

Preferences

Bachelor’s degree in Landscape Architecture or related field of study, plus 1-2 years of professional design experience. Demonstrated ability to read blueprints and building diagrams. Demonstrated proficiency and ability to create and modify documents with AutoCAD, Adobe products (including Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign), Word and Excel. GIS experience preferred.

Demonstrated human relation and effective written and verbal communication skills are required.

Job Summary

The Horticulture Department at Red Butte Garden is looking for a part-time gardener for our greenhouse team. We are seeking someone who wants to work with and learn more about plants and gardening, and enjoys working both indoors in a greenhouse and working outdoors. Would you enjoy learning more about gardening, and working with people who are passionate about plants and nature? Other duties include: make hose repairs as needed, weeding, planting, deadheading, pruning, fertilization, transplanting, sweeping, & event support

Nine months professional work experience or education in a related field preferred, but not required.

Must be able to work 16- 19/hrs week, year-round.
