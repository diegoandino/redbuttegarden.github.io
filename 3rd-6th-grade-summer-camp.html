Calling all actors and actresses! Spend the week exploring drama, dance, visual arts, and more in this FULL-DAY theater camp. Throughout camp, you'll learn to act, create props and backdrops, and work with your fellow thespians to produce and star in a real play. End the week with a live performance of your play in the Garden showcasing all you've learned.
