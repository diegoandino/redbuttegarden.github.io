Classes for children ages 4-12 and a parent.

Each Garden Adventures class focuses on a different topic and seeks to help kids connect with plants while encouraging them to explore the wonders of the natural world.
Classes are designed for parents or caregivers to attend and participate with their child. Limit one adult/caregiver per child.



Garden Adventure class costs - Caregiver included

Garden Members: $8 per child
General Public: $10 per child

Special Garden Adventure classes:

General Public: $15 or $30 per child

PEOPLE AND PLANTS
SATURDAY, APRIL 13

People and Plants

REGISTRATION REQUIRED


SATURDAY, APRIL 13 @ 1 - 2:30PM

{% include register-button.html %}

Ethnobotany is the study of the practical human uses for plants. From helping to keep the air clean to providing delicious snacks, plants are beneficial to us in so many ways! Discover the many unique uses of the plants in the Garden, and later we’ll use your knowledge of ethnobotany to create a simple, delicious treat to share.

SATURDAY, APRIL 27

Earth Superheroes

REGISTRATION REQUIRED


SATURDAY, April 27 @ 1 - 2:30PM

To recycle…and beyond! Join us in the Sprout House for a super Earth Day celebration! Learn how you can be an earth superhero by recycling, conserving, and reusing.
