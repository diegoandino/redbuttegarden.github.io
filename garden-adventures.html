GARDEN ADVENTURES

Kids ages 4-12 and a parent

Garden Adventures are classes for children ages 4-12. Each class focuses on a different topic and seeks to help kids connect with plants while encouraging them to explore the wonders of the natural world. Classes are designed for parents or caregivers to attend and participate with their child. Limit one adult/caregiver per child.

Totally Trees

REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Session A: SATURDAY, APRIL 29 @ 10AM - 11:30AM


Session B: SATURDAY, APRIL 29 @ 1PM - 2:30PM


What do rubber, chocolate, and charcoal all have in common? They all come from trees! Join us as we celebrate Arbor Day (April 28) and investigate the largest residents of the Garden. Together we’ll explore the world of trees and discover a few of the unique varieties found in the Garden, including gingkos, giant sequoias, paperbark maples, and more. At the end of class, we’ll plant live tree seedlings to take home.
