Classes for children ages 4-12 and a parent.

Each Garden Adventures class focuses on a different topic and seeks to help kids connect with plants while encouraging them to explore the wonders of the natural world.
Classes are designed for parents or caregivers to attend and participate with their child. Limit one adult/caregiver per child.
