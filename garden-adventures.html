GARDEN ADVENTURES

For children ages 4-12 and a parent

Each Garden Adventures class focuses on a different topic and seeks to help kids connect with plants
while encouraging them to explore the wonders of the natural world.
Classes are designed for parents or caregivers to attend and participate with their child. Limit one adult/caregiver per child.

REGISTRATION REQUIRED, NO INFANTS PLEASE.

FANTASTIC FALL FOLIAGE
SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 11
Two Sessions

Fantastic Fall Foliage

REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Session A: SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 11 @ 10AM - 11:30AM


Session B: SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 11 @ 1PM - 2:30PM


Have you ever wondered why green leaves change to red, yellow, orange, and brown in the fall? Join us as we investigate this yearly phenomenon and learn why the leaves not only change colors, but also why they fall off the trees. After we'll create pressed leaf animals to help us enjoy the changing seasons.
