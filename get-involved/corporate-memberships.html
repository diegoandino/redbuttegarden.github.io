Corporate Memberships

For 30 years, Red Butte Garden has provided a four-season garden for corporate meetings, luncheons, and retreats. It's a beautiful location for your staff to take a rejuvenating walk through colorful floral paths or peaceful wooded trails.

With a Corporate Membership, you and your staff can enjoy these great benefits and so much more!

  • Free admission for you and your employees for one year
  • Discount on personal Garden memberships
  • Complimentary “Garden After Dark” tickets
  • Discounts on rental fees for corporate events or meetings (based on availability)

Corporate Membership Levels

  • $500     <25 Employees
  • $1000    25-100 Employees
  • $2500    100+ Employees

Horticulture Therapy Pass

Red Butte Garden is a great place to host breakouts and meetings, or simply bring your employees to find a little piece of mind. Horticulture Therapy Passes are perfect for non-profits who frequent the Garden.
