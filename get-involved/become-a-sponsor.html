CORPORATE SPONSORSHIP

Red Butte Garden provides you or your business with a way to participate actively in the cultural life of the community and enjoy an exciting array of benefits acknowledging your support!

Options include corporate membership, special event and concert tickets, receptions and most importantly, name and logo recognition in association with a venue that attracts more than 190,000 people annually.

As a 501 (C)(3) non-profit organization, your generosity helps Red Butte Garden to fulfill our mission to cultivate the human connection with the beauty of living landscapes.

For more information about becoming a sponsor, please contact Wendy Loyning at: Wendy.Loyning@redbutte.utah.edu or (801) 585-5658.
